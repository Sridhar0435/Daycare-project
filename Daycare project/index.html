We love daycare!

Our staff here at Day Care possess the education, experience, training, and enthusiasm to create engaging and positive learning environments. Our staff grows in development right along side the children by attending professional development classes and workshops to keep up with the latest trends in childcare and early education. In each class one or more of the teachers holds either a CDA or a degree in Early Childhood Education. Our teacher to student ratios are kept in line with the State of Illinois standards, allowing our teachers to focus on the individual needs of the children in their care. Our teachers are also available to provide daily feedback on your child’s day, to answer questions, or just to help you meet any unique needs your family may have.

CPE-daycare and RSG-home daycare

CPE-daycare

A daycare centre is a legal person (non-profit organization, cooperative or business corporation), a natural person or an Aboriginal band council providing educational childcare services in a facility. Its board of directors is composed of at least seven members, at least two thirds of whom are user parents or future users of the services provided by the childcare centre (CPE).
